YOUR GENEROSITY FUELS OUR MISSION

Where does the money go?

Your tithes and offerings given to God are used for New Faith Ministries, FOR our neighbors, and others around the globe. We efficiently use your donations to minister at NFM so we can reach those to whom God has called us.

GIVE NOW

Why should I give?

God doesn’t need or want our money; in fact, everything we have comes from God (James 1:17). God wants our hearts, and giving helps us to align our hearts with His (Matthew 6:24). It requires us to trust God to be our ultimate provider (Philippians 4:19), helps us to develop a healthier relationship with money (Matthew 6:19–21), and moves our focus from ourselves and our needs to others and their needs (1 John 3:16–18).

Obedience. We cannot be like Jesus if we are not willing to give like Jesus gave (John 3:16, 1 John 3:16–18).

It’s a tangible way to demonstrate our commitment to God through His church, by allowing us to partner with New Faith Ministries to fund and support the work that God is doing throughout the Columbus area and around the world.
